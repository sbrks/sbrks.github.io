Hi there. I am a technical writer and web developer based in San Francisco, California. I hail from the east coast (born: outside Boston, school: western MA, post-school: NYC), but now call the Bay Area home. I love making things for the web, writing, traveling, reading, exercise, and ice cream.



This Site

Is an effort to consolidate everything I've posted in disparate places over the past seven years. It covers everything from reporting in Brooklyn (2011) to backpacking in South America (2012) all the way to more recent work on design, data visualization, and web development (2016-present).



This site is built on the Jekyll framework with HTML, Sass, YAML, and Javascript. It is served by Github Pages. The design is based off of the Basic Jekyll theme.
